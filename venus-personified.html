Venus Personified

Dec 2024 • 1 min read

Eyes on noisy sky full of stars
Just to spot you closer than mars
Looking for the blaze thus amplified
And there I found my venus personified.

Pale moons across flexing their borrowed glare
You make the sunrays cascade through your hair
While that paleness caters beauty glorified
I found you to be my venus personified.

Through the veils of sulfuric haze
Your resilient gleam radiates the sways
Bypassing you I saw your persona dignified
And there I found my venus personified.

Razing the distance to see you later or soon
I would quit a sky full of pale moons
Levitating the hopes of being unified
I found you to be my venus personified.
